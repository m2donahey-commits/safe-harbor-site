About Safe Harbor

Our Mission

Safe Harbor empowers survivors of domestic abuse and their loved ones by connecting them to trauma-informed resources, education, and compassionate support. Our mission is to create a centralized, safe, and accessible online space where every survivor can find the information, tools, and encouragement needed to begin healing and rebuild with confidence.

Our Vision

Safe Harbor envisions a world where every survivor of domestic abuse can access trauma-informed care without fear, confusion, or barriers â€” a world where safety, understanding, and empowerment are available to all, regardless of circumstance.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe survivors are the experts of their own experiences. Our role is to provide tools, knowledge, and pathways that support healing and self-determination.
  • Compassion: Every element of Safe Harbor reflects empathy, understanding, and respect for the diverse experiences of survivors and their loved ones.
